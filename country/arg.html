Findings

Value

Fostering community trust, social cohesion and civil responsibility through the active engagement of communities is a necessary condition to successfully deliver impact, build resilience and sustainability.

Building community trust though is complex and requires systematic understanding of trust variation and the social, economic, cultural, and environmental factors that influence trust. It requires understanding the root causes of mistrust and the local dynamics of trust.

Without a tool to measure the different dimensions of trust among providers and receivers of services, it is hard for public health and humanitarian responders to take action and create strategies to rebuild and maintain community trust.
