Definition

Overall Score

Overall score is the mean of competencies and values scores. The competencies and values scores are the arithmetic mean of the scores for each sub-dimension. The sub-dimension scores are generated from the weighted average of the response rates to the questions relating to each sub-dimension, using the following weights

Not at allMostly noMostly yesCompletely yes
0123
Neutral responses are not counted.
